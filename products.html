LIFESAVER

The delay in emergency services due to traffic congestion leads to a loss of 30% of lives. Research shows that traditional siren and light systems are effective up to a few meters only.

The product consists of especially designed communication system to enable Emergency Response vehicles to reach their destinations faster. It doesn't require the internet for communication. Tests show up to 50% reduction in Emergency response times. More time is available for the Non-emergency vehicles to give way. It has significantly outperformed the conventional siren-and-light system and can help save many lives by providing medical aid much faster.
